Mauricio de Oliveira

Mauricio de Oliveira is a professor at the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at the University of California, San Diego. His specialty is in the area of Optimization, Dynamic Systems and Control. He has authored over a hundred journal and conference papers on the design of optimal and robust controllers, filtering and state estimation. A common thread in his scientific work is to blend methods from optimization theory with systems and control. He is the author of a book on Tensegrity Structures.
