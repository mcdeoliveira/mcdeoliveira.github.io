Who are we?

VICBee provides consulting services for the finance industry in the areas of mathematical modeling and portfolio optimization. Our focus is on studying and implementing solutions that will match our clients' needs and leverage their existing tools and resources.

What can we do for you?

We can assist you with the following tasks:

  • Develop deterministic and stochastic mathematical models that can be used to
    • optimize asset selection,
    • build risk and cost models,
    • schedule transactions;
  • Write software or guide you in writing your own software that can implement such models in the form of mathematical programs (e.g. linear, quadratic, convex and general nonlinear programs);
  • Help you process and interpret data using techniques such as Kalman filtering;
  • Perform and interpret the result of stochastic simulations.
